Why Tor Matters

  • Anonymity

    Tor is used by political activists, whistleblowers, and journalists around the world who need to shield their identities as they read and write online.

  • Privacy

    Tor helps you hide your IP address and prevent browser fingerprinting, making it more difficult for online trackers and even governments to surveil you.

  • Free Speech

    In countries where huge swaths of the Internet are blocked, people use Tor to access the open, uncensored web.
